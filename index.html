About me

I’m Dhafer Malouche, an Associate Research Scholar at the Department of Statistics and Data Science at Yale University and affiliated with the Whitney and Betty MacMillan Center for International and Area Studies.

I’m also a Professor of Statistics on leave from the University of Carthage in Tunisia.

For my everyday work, I use R, Python, H2O, and Spark. In this website I will be sharing many R and Python codes where I show how one can use these softwares to solve Machine Learning, Statistical Modeling, and Data Visualization problems. I would greatly appreciate all feedback, comments, and questions.
